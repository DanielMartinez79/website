Senior Group Project

The senior group project was a year-long assignmnet which consisted of pitching and idea for a software program, planning, designing and documenting the program, and then finally building it. We went through the entire software engineering process from gathering requirements to launching working software.

  • Cross-platform mobile and web app that could be used by students and teacher to chat during lectures.
  • Gathered requirements, identified use cases and produced requirement analysis document
  • HTML5, CSS3 and Typescript with Ionic framework
  • Agile methodology with daily Scrum meetings
  • Final product can be found at SpeakUp.host
